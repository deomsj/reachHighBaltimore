Erg-A-Thon 2017

Please consider supporting, participating in or cheering on Baltimore Community Rowing’s first annual Erg-a-thon

Why get Involved?

* To raise money for Reach High through this first ever organization-wide event(this is our Gala!)
* To signal the end of winter training (Yippee!) and the start of the spring sprint season
* To bring all of us together in a fun, inspiring event
* To enlist new-comers to our community

Who Can Participate?

Any member of Baltimore Community Rowing, including both youth and adult (Morning Competitive, Day, Open, individual members middle and high school youth)
* Any friend, new-comer or potential youth or adult rower who wants to feel the power and openness of our rowing community

When are where is it taking place?

The erg-a-thon will take place on March 5th all day. It will start at 6 am and end with a team event at 6 pm. This will take place at 3301 Waterview Ave. Baltimore MD, 21230

How does it work?

* Ten Teams with ten people on each team (5 youth, 5 adults)
* Each team raises $2,500 with pledges ($100 per youth, $400 per adult), either on line on each team’s Go-fundme page or on the sponsor sheet
* Each rower ergs for one hour (adults can split the time into 30 min. sessions) from 8:00 am - 6:00 pm (10 hours)
* Celebration/Pot Luck: Party prizes and awards, 6:00 pm-8:00 pm
* Location: Baltimore Rowing Club, upstairs main room
* Total Amount Raised: $25,000
